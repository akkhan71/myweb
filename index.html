Our Story

DIGIX Lab was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between academia and industry by providing high-quality training, development, and research opportunities in Games Development, Animation, VFX, Web Development, AI, and Cybersecurity.

Established under the Air University Business Incubation Center, we aim to nurture talent, foster innovation, and deliver impactful solutions for the digital future.

Vision

To become Pakistan's leading hub for games, animation, and emerging tech development and training, creating a globally competitive workforce.

Mission

Empower learners and organizations through innovative training, cutting-edge research, and industry-grade production services in the digital creative economy.
